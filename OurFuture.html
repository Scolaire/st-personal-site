Suzzanna and Politics

Politics

Politics have been at the fore-front of my life since childhood. Watching President Bill Clinton govern our nation encouraged me to stay informed about our nation's political landscape. His words "Everything has to be paid for" stuck in my mind, and I committed to figuring out how to improve our nation.

Throughout the years I have watched our nation change in many ways, and not always for the better. As I studied civics in high school, I knew our nation needed more than just a reactive leader. We need a proactive representative who has lived our system, experiencing life from the bottom. How many elected politicians do you know that have been homeless, hungry, and struggling? I know of none at this time.

In fact, every elected official and current candidate sounds exactly the same to me. Disconnected from the realities of everyday life, they often fail to address the genuine needs and concerns of the people they represent. What our nation truly needs is leaders who have experienced the struggles of ordinary citizens and can empathize with their challenges. Only then can we hope for meaningful and effective governance.

Leadership

A true representative of the people, is what I strive to be. Not someone who represents only a select few with money and influence. Someone who presents SOLUTIONS that work for ALL of us, instead of compromise. Solutions that benefit the poorest to the wealthiest citizens alike. That mentality is what I have been looking for in a candidate. Since I do not see it in any candidate or elected official, I choose to become what I am searching for in leadership.

Vision for the Future

My vision for the future is one where every citizen has the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances. I believe in policies that promote equality, justice, and sustainable development, ensuring that our nation progresses in a way that benefits all its people.

Leadership should be about service, empathy, and integrity. I am committed to embodying these values and working tirelessly to create a society where everyone has a fair chance to succeed.
